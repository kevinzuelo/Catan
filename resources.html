Resources

Catan has five main resources as its core resources, ore, brick, sheep, wheat, and wood. The European Agricultural Report states that there are 72,900 Hectares of woodland on Catan. We are known for our beautiful forests after all. Along with all of that wood it also states that there were 270,775 tonnes of Green Maize grown across the island.
